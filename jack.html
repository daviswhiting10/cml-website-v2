Head Instructor

Jack
Simon

Jack Simon is an athlete from Westchester, New York where he played both LSM and close defense for the Westlake Wildcats (Thornwood, NY). He attended High Point University, where he graduated in the spring of May 2023 earning a Bachelor of Science in Exercise Science (Kinesiology) and a minor in Computer Science.

Jack has a passion for the game of lacrosse and developing student-athletes to their greatest potential. With years of highly-competitive experience and great mentors he has decided to pass on knowledge for the better development of future generations.

  • 15+ years of playing experience (5 Division I)
  • HPU 2019–2024
  • NY State All-State, All-Region, All-Section, All-County, All-Conference
  • Head Instructor — Central Maryland Lax
