FAQ

Common Questions

My kid already plays for an elite club. Do we need this?

Clubs teach team. We teach position. They’re not the same thing. Your kid can practice with their club Monday and train their position here Saturday.

What if my kid’s more advanced or less advanced than the other kids in the group?

We run two age-grouped time blocks precisely to keep development bands tight. Within each group, every athlete gets direct, individualized feedback — not one-size-fits-all instruction.

Do you cover goalies and face-off specialists?

Not in v1. We focus on Attack, Midfield, LSM, and Close Defense. If you’re a goalie or FOGO parent, email us and we’ll refer you to specialists.

How is this different from a skills camp?

Camps run 60 kids through the same drills. We cap each position group at 25 and run a D1 coach for your kid’s specific position for 75 uninterrupted minutes.

What if we can only make part of the clinic?

Sessions are 75 minutes and structured from start to finish. We recommend attending the full session. If you have a scheduling conflict, reach out before registering.

Some athletes do the minimum and expect maximum results. Elite athletes do the maximum and still ask themselves if they could’ve done more.
