Offensive Instructor

Davis
Whiting

Davis Whiting is a lacrosse instructor and former Division I athlete who brings a player-first perspective to every session. Growing up in an area where lacrosse wasn't part of the mainstream, Davis navigated the recruiting process without a roadmap, learning through trial, effort, and the guidance of great mentors. That experience is the foundation of his coaching philosophy: every athlete's path is different, and the right guidance at the right time makes all the difference.

Davis played four years of Division I lacrosse at High Point University and served as team captain his senior year. He has since applied that competitive experience to coaching both teams and individual players, with a relentless focus on one thing: helping athletes understand exactly what they need to work on and giving them the tools to close that gap at any level, from any background.

  • 4 years Division I lacrosse, High Point University
  • Team Captain, senior year
  • Coached youth and high school athletes across NC, OH, KY, and MD
  • Specialized in offensive player development and recruiting preparation
  • Background helping athletes from non-traditional markets reach the next level
