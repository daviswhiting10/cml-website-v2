Instructors

Head Instructor

Jack
Simon

Former Division I athlete who grew up competing at the highest levels of the sport in New York. Playing LSM and close defense. Jack earned recognition at every level — All-State, All-Region, All-Section, All-County, and All-Conference. His coaching philosophy is rooted in what he learned as a player: elite performance isn't accidental. It's built through deliberate work, great mentorship, and a clear understanding of what separates good athletes from great ones. He's carried that mindset to develop the whole athlete, not just the bare minimum skills.

He has a passion for the game of lacrosse and developing student-athletes to their greatest potential. With years of highly-competitive experience and great mentors he has decided to pass on knowledge for the better development of future generations.

  • 15+ Years of Playing Experience (5 Division I)
  • Athlete Development Lifecycle Focused
  • Coaches Athletes Across NY, NC, & DMV
  • Recruitment Guidance for Underrecognized Athletes
  • Specialist with Defensive Roots
